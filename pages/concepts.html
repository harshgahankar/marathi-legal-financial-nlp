Why These Concepts Matter

Marathi, as a highly agglutinative Indo-Aryan language with rich morphological inflections, presents unique challenges that require specialized NLP techniques. Each concept area builds upon the previous, forming a complete pipeline from raw Devanagari text to production-ready semantic analysis.
