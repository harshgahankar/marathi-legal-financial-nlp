Reflection Note

"Working on the Text Preprocessing and Feature Engineering modules gave me a deep appreciation for the complexities of Marathi as an agglutinative language. Designing the stemming vs lemmatization comparison required careful research into how morphological rules differ for Marathi compared to English. The interactive workflow pipeline helped visualize the sequential nature of preprocessing tasks. This project strengthened my understanding of how low-resource languages demand customized NLP approaches rather than off-the-shelf solutions."

Reflection Note

"Building the Language Representation and Language Models concept cards required understanding how vector semantics apply to Marathi's complex morphology. The Word2Vec vs FastText comparison was particularly insightful — FastText's subword information is far more effective for Marathi due to its agglutinative nature. Implementing the Text-to-Vector pipeline workflow helped me appreciate the transformational journey from discrete tokens to continuous embeddings. This project highlighted the importance of language-specific adaptations in NLP."
