IndicBERT: Large-scale Multilingual Representation

IndicBERT is a multilingual ALBERT model trained on 12 major Indian languages, including Marathi. It leverages the L3Cube-MahaCorpus to achieve superior results in sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, and sentence classification specifically for the Marathi script.

Evolution of Marathi NLP

From rule-based systems to the transformer era

2000s: Statistical Era

HMM-based POS taggers and rule-based morphological analyzers for Marathi.

2014: Word Embeddings

Introduction of Word2Vec and FastText trained on Marathi Wikipedia dumps.

2018: Deep Learning

RNN and LSTM models revolutionizing Marathi machine translation.

2021-Present: Transformers

BERT, GPT-3, and Indic-specific LLMs mastering Marathi nuances.
